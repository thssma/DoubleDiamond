# MVP Scope

This file defines the focused navigation and product surface for Block 2.

## Client

Primary goal: follow project progress and retrieve reports.

Visible routes:

- Client Portal
- Reports

Deferred/protected routes:

- Work Orders
- Payments
- Messages
- Internal operations and administration

## Employee

Primary goal: execute field work.

Visible routes:

- Home
- Work Orders
- Routes
- Field
- Workforce
- Weather
- Mobile
- PWA
- Reports

Deferred/protected routes:

- Finance
- Executive dashboards
- AI/automation labs
- Administration

## Owner

Primary goal: manage operation and business performance without prototype noise.

Visible routes:

- Home
- Clients
- Work Orders
- Routes
- Field
- Workforce
- Weather
- Reports
- Profitability
- BI Dashboard
- Executive
- Integrations
- Settings

Deferred/prototype routes:

- AI Foundation
- AI Command
- AI Leads
- AI Quotes
- AI risk/advisor/planner pages
- Automation lab pages
- Role demo pages
- Raw channel simulators unless promoted in a later block

## Block 2 Rule

Do not delete deferred screens yet. Hide them from MVP navigation and keep the code available until Product/UX review confirms removal or promotion.
